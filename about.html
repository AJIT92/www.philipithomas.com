About

I study at Washington University in St. Louis, and in Spring 2013 I will graduate with majors in Systems Engineering and Physics, and with a minor in Electrical Engineering. After graduation, I intend to work in software engineering, and I currently seek employment opportunities. I serve as an Analyst on the technical due diligence team at Cultivation Capital. My recent projects include Text Reject, Food Trucks Near Me, and Automation Kit. Press coverage for these and other projects is aggregated here. I dance on the Salsa performance team WUSauce, and I run wute.vc, a venture-capital lecture series in the Washington University Tech Entrepreneurs club. Other interests include coffee, beer, and rock climbing.
