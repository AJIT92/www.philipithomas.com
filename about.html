About

I study at Washington University in St. Louis, and this May I graduate with majors in Systems Engineering and Physics, and with a minor in Electrical Engineering. After graduation, am moving to San Francisco to join OpenDNS as a Software Engineer.

I serve as an Analyst on the technical due diligence team at Cultivation Capital. My articles have been published in the St. Louis Business Journal and the Rackspace Blog. My recent projects include Text Reject, Sidecar Speakeasy, and Food Trucks Near Me. I also work as a freelance developer, and I contribute to the open-source community.

I dance on the Salsa performance team WUSauce, and I run wute.vc, a venture-capital lecture series in the Washington University Tech Entrepreneurs club. Other interests include coffee, beer, and rock climbing.
